Passion and Aim

I have always been passionate about working with children, first as a teacher and now as a counsellor. I am also passionate about working with women of all ages. I have a desire to help mothers of all ages, but especially teen mothers and mothers of children with special needs.

My aim is to get to know you as an individual and to explore your world in a way that sits comfortably with you. I believe that a positive therapeutic relationship is important if you are to achieve your goals. I endeavour to listen with sensitivity, setting goals together and using a variety of therapeutic techniques to help you achieve your goals.

I am a Christian, so I am happy to include Biblical worldview throughout sessions, however I will only do this if you desire it.

About Us

Consulting Hours: Tuesday – Thursday (9am-2pm)
Mobile Service: South East Brisbane Area

  • I can meet you in a location that is convenient for you or your child if it is safe and appropriate for counselling. If your child’s school is welcoming then I am happy to go to your child’s school.
  • I also work out of Christian Heritage College counselling rooms if that is convenient for you.

Length of Sessions: approx. 50 mins (may be shortened for children if necessary)
Consultation Fees: start from $50 depending on length of sessions and travel distance
Payment Options: Cash or Internet Transfer once invoice has been issued
Cancellations: 24 hours notice of a cancellation is appreciated, otherwise fees may still be charged

Services

Children's Issues

  • Anxiety
  • Depression
  • Bullying issues
  • High functioning autism
  • Attention Deficit Hyperactivity Disorder (ADHD)
  • Friendship issues
  • Trauma
  • Abuse issues
  • Grief
  • High Functioning Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD) strengths and challenges

I generally use play therapy, expressive therapies and Cognitive Behavioural Therapy when working with children. It depends on the presenting issues and on the preference of the child. Some children seem to prefer one type of therapy over another or find one method works better for them.

Women's Issues

  • Anxiety
  • Depression
  • Relationship issues
  • Self-esteem
  • Trauma
  • Abuse issues
  • Personal issues
  • Grief
  • Parenting

I use a range of therapies depending on the desires of the client and on the issues being presented. I engage in a conversational manner, recognising you as the expert in your world. Some techniques I use are talk therapy, expressive therapies and Cognitive Behavioural Therapy.
