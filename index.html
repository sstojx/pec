Passion and Aim

From my early adult years, my heart’s desire was to work with children to help them attain their full potential. I set out to achieve this by becoming a Primary School Teacher, a hands-on job that I loved. After several years of teaching, I realised that there were many complexities in the lives of the children and their families that I did not want to ignore, but I was not qualified to help them. This experience, along with becoming a parent myself, led me to pursue counselling as a profession.

Having attained my Master of Counselling degree, I would now love to be able to assist those who are reaching out for a caring and helping heart and mind. My passion and desire to work with children has never diminished, but I also enjoy counselling women of all ages, helping them to deal with life’s challenges and celebrating life’s victories. Having experience parenting children with special needs, I know I have the understanding and capacity to help all mothers, but especially those who have children with special needs.

My aim is to get to know you as an individual and to explore your world in a way that sits comfortably with you. I believe that a positive therapeutic relationship is important if you are to achieve your goals. I endeavour to listen with sensitivity, setting goals together and using a variety of therapeutic techniques to help you achieve your goals.

I am a Christian, so I am happy to include Biblical perspectives throughout sessions if you desire and consent to this. I am also happy to conduct all sessions without Biblical reference if that is your preference.
